Your Software as a Solution
Pathways easy to use software tools help colleges automate their matriculation process, student services verification and increases visibility and efficiencies across all student service programs. Pathways increases a college's ability to guide more students through the system and improve post-secondary student outcomes.

Pathways

Structured Academic Pathways
Pathways transforms the entire student experience by helping them identify their ideal career path and stay on that path through completion and transition to the workforce or a four-year institution..
Pathways is designed to close achievement gaps and accelerate success among diverse student populations, particularly low-income students and students of color.

  • Cloud Infrastructure

    Flexible cloud-based technology designed to lower the cost of maintaining campus systems.

  • Data Integration
